## 3. Security Questionnaire (30 Questions)

### Access Control
1. Is MFA enforced across all environments?  
2. Are privileged accounts monitored?  
3. Are access reviews done quarterly?  

### Data Protection
4. Is data encrypted (transit + rest)?  
5. Do you follow a data retention policy?  
6. Are backups encrypted and tested?  

### Infrastructure Security
7. Do you run endpoint protection?  
8. Are weekly vulnerability scans performed?  
9. How fast do you patch critical vulnerabilities?  

### Network Security
10. Do you use firewalls/WAF?  
11. Is network traffic continuously monitored?  

### Cloud Security
12. Do you restrict public access?  
13. Are secrets/keys rotated regularly?  

### Incident Response
14. Do you maintain an IR plan?  
15. Do you notify customers of incidents?  
16. What is your average MTTR?  

### Compliance
17. Are you SOC2 / ISO27001 compliant?  
18. Do you sign a DPA?  
19. Do you provide audit reports?  

### Privacy
20. Do you track geographic data storage?  
21. Do you support data deletion requests?  

### Operational
22. What is your uptime SLA?  
23. Do you maintain a BCP/DRP?  
24. Do you test failovers annually?  

### HR & Internal Controls
25. Are employees background-checked?  
26. Is annual security training mandatory?  

### Software Development
27. Do you run code reviews?  
28. Do you perform penetration testing?  
29. Do you use SAST/DAST?  
30. Is security integrated into SDLC?
